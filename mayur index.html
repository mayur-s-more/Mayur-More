Our Core Purpose

Mission

YUVA BHARAT focuses on uplifting youth through various social programs. By engaging young individuals in community projects, it aims to create a more participative and informed society.

Overview

We are committed to empowering the youth of Mumbai through social and educational initiatives, fostering their potential and impactful engagement in society.

What is Abhyasika?

Our flagship initiative is a systematic, five-step process designed to provide foundational support where it's needed most.

1

Identifying Vasti

Locating communities in need of educational intervention.

2

Survey

Conducting surveys to understand specific educational gaps.

3

Identifying Students

Enrolling students who require academic assistance.

4

Daily Tuitions

Providing consistent, quality tutoring to improve learning outcomes.

5

Resource Funding

Securing NGO funds for tuition fees and basic stationary.
